Version: 1.0

# Purpose

This document defines the Graph Visualization subsystem of the Zanistarast Visualization platform.

The Graph Visualization subsystem provides deterministic, certified, and reproducible visualization of knowledge graphs, dependency graphs, ontologies, semantic networks, and relational structures while preserving compatibility with the Certified Core and Rasterast Mathematics.

Every graph visualization shall be deterministic, reproducible, explainable, certifiable, and fully auditable.
