# Purpose

This document defines the Notebook Language of the Zanistarast Research Notebook.

The Notebook Language provides deterministic, certified, and reproducible representation of scientific computation, documentation, experiments, mathematical reasoning, and executable research workflows while preserving compatibility with the Certified Core and Rasterast Mathematics.

Every notebook shall be deterministic, reproducible, explainable, certifiable, and fully auditable.
