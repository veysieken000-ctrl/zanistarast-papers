# Purpose

This document defines the Reproducibility subsystem of the Zanistarast Research Notebook.

The Reproducibility subsystem provides deterministic, certified, and reproducible execution of scientific experiments, computational notebooks, and formal research workflows while preserving compatibility with the Certified Core and Rasterast Mathematics.

Every reproducibility operation shall be deterministic, reproducible, explainable, certifiable, and fully auditable.
