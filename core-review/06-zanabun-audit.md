# Working Definition

Zanabûn denotes certified knowledge.

Knowledge is considered Zanabûn only when it refers to a certified existence (Hebûn), preserves deterministic identity (Yek), remains consistent with unified existence (Vahid), and ultimately depends upon the primitive certainty established by Ehad.

Knowledge that cannot be deterministically verified is not certified Zanabûn.
