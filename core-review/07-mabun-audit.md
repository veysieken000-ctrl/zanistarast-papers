# Working Definition

Mabûn denotes certified preservation.

Knowledge becomes Mabûn only when certified knowledge (Zanabûn) remains deterministically preserved without violating its dependency chain.

Preservation includes integrity,

continuity,

consistency,

and resistance to unauthorized modification.

Knowledge that cannot preserve its certified integrity cannot be considered Mabûn.
