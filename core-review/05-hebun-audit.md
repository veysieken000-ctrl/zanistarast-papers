# Working Definition

Hebûn denotes certified existence.

An entity is considered to possess Hebûn only when its existence is deterministically identifiable through its Yek and remains consistent with the ontological foundation established by Ehad and Vahid.

Existence without deterministic identity cannot be certified as Hebûn.
