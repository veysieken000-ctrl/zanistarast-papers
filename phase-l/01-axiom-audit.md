# Purpose

This document establishes the foundational axioms of the Zanistarast Scientific Synthesis.

These axioms define the permanent principles upon which all future mathematical, scientific, computational, social, ethical, and technological developments shall be constructed.

Every future theorem, model, experiment, software component, and scientific claim shall remain compatible with these axioms.

---

# Axiom 1
## Reality Exists

Reality exists independently of human opinion.

Truth is discovered rather than created.

---

# Axiom 2
## Truth Is One

Truth cannot contradict itself.

Apparent contradictions arise from

• incomplete knowledge,

• incorrect assumptions,

• measurement limitations,

• interpretation errors.

---

# Axiom 3
## Knowledge Evolves

Human knowledge is always incomplete.

Every discovery may improve understanding.

No scientific model is considered absolutely complete.

---

# Axiom 4
## Verification Is Continuous

Verification never ends.

Every scientific statement remains open to continual examination through the Zanistarast verification methodology.

---

# Axiom 5
## Rasterast Verification

Every scientific statement shall pass the Rasterast Verification Filter before becoming certified knowledge.

Certification is always reproducible.

---

# Axiom 6
## Hebûn Compatibility

Every scientific model shall preserve ontological consistency.

False entities shall not become permanent components of the knowledge system.

---

# Axiom 7
## Zanabûn Compatibility

Scientific understanding shall always seek broader, deeper and more comprehensive perspectives while remaining consistent with verified reality.

---

# Axiom 8
## Integration Principle

Any verified discovery, method, theorem, observation or scientific technique that passes the Rasterast Verification Filter and is compatible with Hebûn and Zanabûn principles becomes part of the Zanistarast Scientific Synthesis regardless of its origin.

Knowledge belongs to truth rather than civilizations.

---

# Axiom 9
## Error Is Temporary

Errors are temporary.

Verified truth becomes permanent until improved by stronger verified evidence.

---

# Axiom 10
## Scientific Humility

Every conclusion remains open to better evidence.

Scientific certainty belongs only to fully verified knowledge.
