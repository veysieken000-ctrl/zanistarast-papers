# BOOK IA — Mathematical Validation

Version 1.0

---

# Purpose

Book IA establishes the mathematical validation of the Zanistarast framework.

Book I introduced the mathematical foundation.

Book IA evaluates whether that foundation is internally consistent, mathematically rigorous and suitable for future formal verification.

---

# Validation Roadmap

## Phase 1

Consistency Analysis

Determine whether the primitive definitions, axioms and derived results are mutually compatible.

---

## Phase 2

Soundness Analysis

Determine whether every derivable statement follows from the axioms.

---

## Phase 3

Completeness Analysis

Determine the expressive limits of the current axiomatic system.

---

## Phase 4

Model Existence

Investigate whether mathematical models satisfying the axioms exist.

---

## Phase 5

Independence of Axioms

Determine whether every axiom is necessary.

---

## Phase 6

Minimal Axiom Set

Construct the smallest sufficient axiomatic basis.

---

## Phase 7

Traceability Analysis

Verify that every theorem depends only upon previously established results.

## Phase 8

Limitations

# Scientific Goal

Book IA does not introduce new mathematical concepts.

It evaluates the correctness of the mathematical foundations established in Book I.
