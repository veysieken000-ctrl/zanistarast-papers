# Chapter 07 — Traceability Analysis

Version 1.0

---

# Purpose

This chapter establishes complete traceability between every mathematical result and its dependencies.

Every mathematical statement shall be traceable to

• primitive definitions

• axioms

• inference rules

• previous lemmas

• previous theorems

No hidden dependency is permitted.

---

# Traceability Matrix

Every theorem shall contain

Definitions

Axioms

Inference Rules

Lemmas

Theorems

used during derivation.

---

# Dependency Graph

The dependency graph must remain acyclic.

Circular derivations are not permitted.

---

# Scientific Review

Traceability guarantees reproducibility and mathematical transparency.
