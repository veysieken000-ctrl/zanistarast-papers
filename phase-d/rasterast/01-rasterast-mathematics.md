# Purpose

This document establishes the mathematical foundation of Rasterast Mathematics.

Rasterast Mathematics studies deterministic verification, certification, consistency validation, trust evaluation, and final correctness of certified systems while preserving compatibility with the Certified Core.

It extends Rabûn Mathematics by transforming governed systems into mathematically verified and certifiable systems.
