# Theorem 1

Integrity Preservation Theorem

Statement

Every certified Hebûn Operator preserves the integrity of every certified Hebûn Element.

Consequence

Persistence Theorem

Statement

Consequence

Certified history always remains reconstructable.

Stability Theorem

Statement

Repeated certified operations preserve the stability of certified Hebûn Elements.

Consequence

# Theorem 5

Certified Closure Theorem

Statement

Every certified Hebûn operation produces another certified Hebûn Element whenever preservation constraints remain satisfied.

Consequence

Recovery Compatibility Theorem

Consequence

Identical certified inputs processed through identical certified Hebûn Operators always produce identical certified outputs.

Consequence

Preservation is reproducible.
