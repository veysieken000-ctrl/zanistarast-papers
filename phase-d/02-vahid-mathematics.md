# Vahid Mathematics

Version: 1.0

Status: Research

---

# Purpose

This document establishes the mathematical foundation of Vahid Mathematics.

It is the first Mathematical Extension built upon the Certified Core v1.0.

Vahid Mathematics studies mathematical structures that preserve ontological unity while remaining compatible with the Primitive Chain and Rasterast Verification.

---

# Scope

Vahid Mathematics defines

• Vahid Space

• Vahid Operators

• Vahid Relations

• Vahid Metrics

• Vahid Transformations

• Vahid Algebra

Future versions may extend these concepts without modifying the Certified Core.

# Fundamental Principle

Every mathematical object within Vahid Mathematics shall preserve ontological unity.

If a transformation destroys certified unity, it is outside the scope of Vahid Mathematics.
