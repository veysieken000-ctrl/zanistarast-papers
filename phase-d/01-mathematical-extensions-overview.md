# Mathematical Extensions

Version: 1.0

Status: Research

---

# Purpose

This document defines the Mathematical Extensions phase of the Zanistarast framework.

The Certified Core v1.0 establishes the immutable mathematical foundation.

Mathematical Extensions expand that foundation without modifying certified primitives.

---

# Principles

Every extension shall

• preserve Certified Core,

• preserve deterministic reasoning,

• preserve dependency order,

• remain compatible with Rasterast verification,

• avoid introducing contradictions.
