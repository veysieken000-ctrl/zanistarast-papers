# Proof 1

Identity Preservation Theorem

Premises

• Definition of Vahid Element

• Definition of Identity Operator

• Certified Operator Rules

Derivation

2. Apply the Identity Operator I.

3. By definition,

I(v)=v.

4. Therefore the certified identity of v remains unchanged.

Conclusion

Identity is preserved under the Identity Operator.
