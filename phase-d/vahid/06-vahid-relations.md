# Equality Relation

Notation

=

Definition

Two elements are equal only if

• identity is preserved,

• dependency chain is identical,

• certification status is identical.

---

# Dependency Relation

Notation

≺

Definition

v₁ ≺ v₂

means

v₂ depends upon v₁.

Properties

• asymmetric

• transitive

• acyclic

---

# Containment Relation

Notation

⊂

Definition

v₁ ⊂ v₂

means

v₁ is a certified component of v₂.

Containment shall never destroy identity.

---

# Compatibility Relation

Notation

≈

Definition

v₁ ≈ v₂

means

both elements may participate in a certified operation together.

Compatibility does not imply equality.

---

# Certification Relation

Notation

⊨

Definition

Core ⊨ v

means

the element satisfies every certification rule defined by the Certified Core.

---

# Relation Properties

Every certified relation shall satisfy

• deterministic interpretation,

• dependency preservation,

• reproducibility,

• Rasterast compatibility.

---

# Forbidden Relations

Relations are invalid if they

• introduce circular dependency,

• violate certified identity,

• contradict primitive definitions,

• bypass Rasterast verification.

---

# Future Extensions

Future research may define

• ordering relations,

• partial orders,

• category relations,

provided they remain compatible with the Certified Core.
