# Governance Relation

Notation

≡G

Definition

Two certified governance systems are related if they preserve identical governance objectives while maintaining deterministic operational behavior.

---

# Coordination Relation

Notation

≈C

Definition

Two governance systems are coordination-equivalent whenever they produce identical certified coordination despite differences in internal implementation.

---

# Control Relation

Notation

⇢K

Definition

A control relation specifies how one certified governance element supervises another while preserving operational consistency and dependency integrity.

# Sustainability Relation

Notation

⇢S

Definition

Notation

⊨

# Relation Properties

Every certified Rabûn Relation shall preserve

• deterministic governance,

• operational consistency,

• dependency continuity,

• sustainability,

• Rasterast compatibility.

---

# Forbidden Relations

Relations are prohibited if they

• introduce governance conflicts,

• create dependency cycles,

• invalidate certification,

• destroy operational continuity,

• produce uncertified governance behavior.
