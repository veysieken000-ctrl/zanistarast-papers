# Citation Policy

Version: 1.0

Status: Architecture

# Purpose

This document defines the Citation Policy of the Zanistarast Academic Ecosystem.

The Citation Policy provides deterministic, certified, and reproducible management of scientific references, citations, attribution, and dependency relationships while preserving compatibility with the Certified Core and Rasterast Mathematics.

Every citation shall be deterministic, reproducible, explainable, certifiable, and fully auditable.
