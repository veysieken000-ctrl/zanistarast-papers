# Purpose

This document defines the Book Series Architecture of the Zanistarast Academic Ecosystem.

The Book Series Architecture provides deterministic, certified, and reproducible organization of books, volumes, chapters, references, revisions, and long-term preservation while preserving compatibility with the Certified Core and Rasterast Mathematics.

Every book shall be deterministic, reproducible, explainable, certifiable, and fully auditable.
