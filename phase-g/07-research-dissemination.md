# Purpose

This document defines the Research Dissemination subsystem of the Zanistarast Academic Ecosystem.

The Research Dissemination subsystem provides deterministic, certified, and reproducible distribution of scientific knowledge across academic, industrial, governmental, and public communication channels while preserving compatibility with the Certified Core and Rasterast Mathematics.

Every dissemination activity shall be deterministic, reproducible, explainable, certifiable, and fully auditable.
