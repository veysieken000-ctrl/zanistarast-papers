# Research Hypothesis — Universal Simulation

## Hypothesis

If the Zanistarast framework correctly captures the structural principles of a system, then the same deterministic framework should be applicable to the analysis of systems regardless of their temporal position (past, present, or future) or disciplinary domain, provided that the relevant assumptions and data are available.

## Interpretation

The framework is intended to model structural relationships rather than a specific historical period or scientific discipline.

Consequently, the same formal framework may be tested against:

- natural systems,
- historical systems,
- social systems,
- legal systems,
- biological systems,
- computational systems,

using identical mathematical principles.

## Research Goal

Evaluate whether the framework provides consistent and reproducible results across different domains through simulation and formal verification.

## Scientific Status

This is a research hypothesis.

It is not currently presented as a proven theorem.
