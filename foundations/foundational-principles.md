# Foundational Principles of Zanistarast

Version: 1.0

---

## Principle 1 — Scientific Fidelity

The purpose of this project is to formalize the Zanistarast scientific synthesis without altering its conceptual identity.

---

## Principle 2 — Mathematical Rigor

Every concept shall be expressed with the highest achievable mathematical precision.

---

## Principle 3 — Deterministic Development

Every result shall be reproducible under identical assumptions.

---

## Principle 4 — Conceptual Preservation

Mathematics serves the framework.

The framework shall not be arbitrarily modified to fit an existing mathematical formalism.

---

## Principle 5 — Yek–Vahid Principle

Every created entity possesses a unique Yek.

The totality of all created Yek constitutes Vahid.

The purpose of the Zanistarast framework is to formalize the deterministic order connecting Yek and Vahid through

Hebûn,

Zanabûn,

Mabûn,

Rabûn,

and Rasterast.

---

## Principle 6 — Scientific Expansion

Future mathematical developments shall extend the framework without contradicting these foundational principles.
