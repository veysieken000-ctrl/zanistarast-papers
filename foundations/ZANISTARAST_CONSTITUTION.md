# ZANISTARAST SCIENTIFIC CONSTITUTION

Version: 1.0

---

# Mission

The purpose of the Zanistarast project is not to modify its own scientific synthesis.

Its purpose is to rigorously formalize, verify, and mathematically develop the Zanistarast scientific synthesis while preserving its internal coherence.

Every mathematical definition, theorem, proof, algorithm and formalization must remain consistent with the framework.

---

# Scientific Principle

The mathematical development exists to evaluate, formalize and strengthen the Zanistarast framework.

It shall never intentionally contradict the framework's core principles.

If inconsistencies are discovered, they shall be resolved by:

- refining definitions,
- refining notation,
- refining axioms,
- refining proofs,

without arbitrarily changing the conceptual identity of the framework.

# Foundational Perspective

Within the Zanistarast framework, the Qur'an is treated as the primary source describing universal order.

Accordingly, the framework studies universal order through the concepts of law, order, ethics, knowledge and society as understood within its own internal perspective.

This statement defines the project's internal research perspective.

It is not presented as a mathematical theorem.

---

# Methodological Rule

Mathematics serves the framework.

The framework is not modified merely to satisfy an existing mathematical formalism.

Instead,

definitions,

axioms,

notation,

proofs,

and formal systems

shall be refined until the framework is expressed with maximum mathematical rigor while preserving its conceptual identity.

---

# Permanent Rule

Scientific rigor and conceptual fidelity shall always progress together.

Neither may be sacrificed for the other.

The objective is not merely mathematical elegance.

The objective is the most rigorous possible formalization of the Zanistarast scientific synthesis.
