# Purpose

This document defines the Versioning Policy of the Zanistarast Community & Governance framework.

The Versioning Policy provides deterministic, certified, and reproducible management of releases, compatibility, deprecation, and long-term maintenance while preserving compatibility with the Certified Core and Rasterast Mathematics.

Every version transition shall be deterministic, reproducible, explainable, certifiable, and fully auditable.

# Semantic Versioning

Every official release follows semantic versioning.

MAJOR.MINOR.PATCH

where

• MAJOR introduces certified architectural changes,

• MINOR introduces backward-compatible functionality,

• PATCH introduces certified corrections without architectural changes.

Version identifiers are immutable after release.
