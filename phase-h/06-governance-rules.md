# Purpose

This document defines the Governance Rules of the Zanistarast Community & Governance framework.

The Governance Rules provide deterministic, certified, and reproducible governance for the Zanistarast ecosystem while preserving compatibility with the Certified Core and Rasterast Mathematics.

Every governance decision shall be deterministic, reproducible, explainable, certifiable, and fully auditable.
