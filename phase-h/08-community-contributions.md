# Purpose

This document defines the Community Contributions framework of the Zanistarast Community & Governance architecture.

The Community Contributions framework provides deterministic, certified, and reproducible participation for individuals, institutions, and organizations contributing to the Zanistarast ecosystem while preserving compatibility with the Certified Core and Rasterast Mathematics.

Every contribution shall be deterministic, reproducible, explainable, certifiable, and fully auditable.
