# Purpose

This document defines the Change Management framework of the Zanistarast Community & Governance architecture.

The Change Management framework provides deterministic, certified, and reproducible management of all architectural, software, scientific, academic, and governance changes while preserving compatibility with the Certified Core and Rasterast Mathematics.

Every change shall be deterministic, reproducible, explainable, certifiable, and fully auditable.
