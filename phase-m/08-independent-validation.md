# Independent Validation Framework

Version: 2.0 Draft

Status: Formal Science

---

# Purpose

This document establishes the Independent Validation Framework of the Zanistarast Scientific Synthesis.

The objective is to ensure that scientific conclusions remain independently reproducible, auditable and verifiable.

Independent validation strengthens scientific confidence through transparent verification.

---

# Fundamental Principle

Scientific verification shall not depend upon a single researcher, institution or implementation.

Independent verification strengthens certified knowledge.

---

# Independent Validation Workflow

Certified Scientific Object

↓

Independent Reviewer

↓

Independent Verification

↓

Evidence Comparison

↓

Rasterast Verification

↓

Validation Report

↓

Scientific Registry

---

# Validation Scope

Independent validation may evaluate

• definitions,

• axioms,

• mathematical proofs,

• experiments,

• benchmarks,

• datasets,

• algorithms,

• publications,

• software implementations.

---

# Validation Requirements

Every independent validation shall include

• original scientific object,

• verification methodology,

• reproduced evidence,

• observed results,

• documented differences,

• final evaluation.

---

# Validation Outcomes

Every validation produces one of the following results

• Confirmed

• Confirmed with Notes

• Partially Confirmed

• Requires Revision

• Not Confirmed

---

# Scientific Integrity

Independent validation shall remain

• objective,

• transparent,

• reproducible,

• traceable,

• evidence-based.

Reviewer identity shall never replace scientific evidence.

---
