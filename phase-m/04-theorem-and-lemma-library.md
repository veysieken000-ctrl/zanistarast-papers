# Purpose

This document establishes the official Theorem and Lemma Library of the Zanistarast Scientific Synthesis.

The library provides a deterministic and certified repository of mathematical knowledge.

Every theorem shall be traceable to explicit definitions, certified axioms, verified lemmas and documented proofs.

---

# Fundamental Principle

No theorem exists independently.

Every theorem depends upon

• certified definitions,

• certified axioms,

• certified lemmas,

• certified proofs.

Every dependency shall remain traceable.

# Definitions

Definitions establish scientific meaning.

Definitions do not require proof.

Definitions shall remain internally consistent.

---

# Axioms

Axioms establish the permanent foundations of the scientific framework.

Every theorem ultimately depends upon certified axioms.

---

# Lemmas

A lemma is an intermediate verified result.

Lemmas simplify future proofs.

A certified lemma may be referenced by multiple theorems.

---

# Propositions

A proposition is a scientific statement awaiting complete verification.

A proposition becomes a theorem only after successful certification.

---

# Theorems

A theorem is a scientifically certified statement derived through deterministic proof.

Every theorem requires

• referenced definitions,

• referenced axioms,

• referenced lemmas,

• deterministic proof,

• Rasterast Certification.

---

# Corollaries

A corollary is a direct consequence of one or more certified theorems.

Additional proof may not be necessary if the dependency chain remains complete.

---

# Dependency Graph

Every mathematical object records

• parent definitions,

• parent axioms,

• parent lemmas,

• parent theorems,

• proof history,

• certification history.

The dependency graph shall remain immutable after certification.
