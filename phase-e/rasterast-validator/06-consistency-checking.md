# Purpose

This document defines the Consistency Checking subsystem of the Zanistarast Rasterast Validator.

The Consistency Checking subsystem ensures that every certified object, mathematical definition, ontology, dependency graph, execution plan, and reasoning chain remains structurally, semantically, mathematically, and operationally consistent while preserving compatibility with the Certified Core and Rasterast Mathematics.

Every consistency evaluation shall be deterministic, reproducible, explainable, certifiable, and fully auditable.
