The Proof Engine is responsible for

• axiom interpretation,

# Proof Construction

Proof construction consists of

• Certified Axioms

• Certified Definitions

• Verified Lemmas

• Inference Rules

• Theorem Statements

• Proof Steps

Each proof step is independently verifiable.

---

# Axiom Evaluation

Every proof begins with certified axioms.

Each axiom is evaluated for

• consistency,

• completeness,

• dependency integrity,

• semantic correctness,

• Rasterast compatibility.

Only certified axioms may participate in proofs.

---

# Theorem Verification

Every theorem is verified through

• logical correctness,

• deterministic inference,

• dependency preservation,

• proof completeness,

• certification continuity.

Incomplete proofs are rejected.

• Verification Status

• Certification Timestamp

• Audit Reference
